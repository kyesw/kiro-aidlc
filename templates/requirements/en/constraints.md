## Features NOT to Implement

### Payment (Mandatory Exclusion)
- Actual payment processing (card, cash, digital wallet)
- Payment gateway integration (PG provider)
- Receipt issuance and printing
- Refund processing

### Authentication/Security (Mandatory Exclusion)
- Complex user authentication (OAuth, social login)
- Multi-factor authentication (2FA, OTP)
- Personal data encryption and GDPR compliance
